About PowerHouse Gym

PowerHouse Gym has been a leader in fitness since 1990, delivering exceptional training environments, world-class equipment, and expert trainers. Our mission is to empower every individual to achieve their health and fitness goals through personalized support, innovative programs, and a strong sense of community. Whether you're just beginning or you're an experienced athlete, PowerHouse Gym provides the tools, motivation, and support you need to transform your body and mind.
